Vmojin



Vmoji is an emoji-based video chat application that reads and imitates the facial expressions by the user. This app was implemented with Facial Dynamics technology, which was developed during the development of the Digital Human by Reality Reflection.

About

The cute emoji tracks the facial expressions such as cheek bloating, winking, frowning, and more. Users can create and record hilarious videos with Vmoji, and can also access random video chatting with other users in real time.
