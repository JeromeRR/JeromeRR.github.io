Features

  • Own your landmark

    Mossland is a location-based AR (Augmented Reality) mobile game. You can find and check-in to the buildings and properties around you. Enjoy the game and get a chance to own Eiffel Tower, Big Ben, Golden Gate Bridge and more!

  • Add accessory to your property

    Do you want more visitors to your properties? You can add attractive AR Objects to your properties. They will make your properties unique and fun!

  • Host advertising on your properties

    Mossland provides totally new concept of location-based advertisement. You can make your properties a popular ad spot to earn money. If you are an advertiser, you can attract nearby customers with impressive AR advertisement!

  • Trade your properties and earn money

    Are you a successful landlord of a famous Landmark? Trade your properties to earn Moss Coin (Ethereum-based cryptocurrency) and to cash out through Cryptocurrency Exchange in the future!
