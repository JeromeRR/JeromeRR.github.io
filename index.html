Features

  • Own Your Landmark

    Mossland is a location based AR mobile game. You can find and check in the properties around your real location. You can be the owner of those properties by gameplay. Own Eiffel Tower, Big Ben and Golden Gate Bridge. It’s up to you.

  • Craft Your Properties

    How could you make your landmarks more fantastic? You can craft your properties by adding ACCESSARY – Augmented Reality Object. Let’s make our land to be more beautiful and funny!

  • Attract advertising on your place

    Moss Land is a location based advertisement platform with a new concept. You can make your property a hot ad spot and earn real money from it. If you are an advertiser, you can make impressive AR ad on your city.

  • Sell Your Landmark and Earn Real Money

    Have you succeeded in making your properties a great hot spot? Sell your property at an expensive price and get Moss Coins (Ethereum based cryptocurrency). You can earn even real money by trading Moss Coin.
