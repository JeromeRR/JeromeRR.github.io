Speedball Arena



Speedball Arena is a VR sports game that users can be the heroic athlete of the futuristic sports on a lightspeed hoverbike. Supporting 2:2 multiplayer mode, players get on hover bikes hook the ball to score in opponent’s goal.

About

Speed in Speedball Arena is a huge difference compared to other VR titles. The game is getting more popular among VR arcade chains in North America where the team concentrates to expand the business.
