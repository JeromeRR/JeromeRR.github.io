Dancing Zena



Dancing Zena is world's first tech-demo to showcase how delicate and realistic a dancing digital human can be. Using Epic's Unreal Engine, the project renders a representation of a girl, Zena at 90 frames per second. This could be realized with the studio's 3D art techniques and 3D scanning technology based on our 3D Scanning studio, which is the biggest in Asia with 160 DSLRs, and the collaboration with major partner companies such as 3Lateral, which is known for high-quality face scans and rigs.

About Zena

Not only does Zena perfectly follow the rhythm of the song with her movements, she also makes eye contact with the user, something that is made possible by using eye-tracking to follow the user’s gaze.
